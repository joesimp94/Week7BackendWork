About Pikachu's Prose Palace

I decided to open a bookshop after a mishap involving my short-lived career as a Pokémon trainer. My so-called "legendary" team of Pokémon turned out to be anything but legendary, and my battles were more like slapstick comedy routines. After one particularly embarrassing defeat to a rookie trainer, I realized I might have better luck with books than with Poké Balls. Now, my bookshop's mascot, a Pikachu named "Bookachu," occasionally shocks customers with its love for literature instead of battling other Pokémon. Who knew that the pages of books could provide a more exciting adventure than the world of Pokémon battles?
